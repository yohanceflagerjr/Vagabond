# Vagabond ♟

> *A personal productivity app built around your life — not the other way around.*

Vagabond is a private, offline-first productivity app for people who want to stay organized without giving their data to the cloud. Everything lives on your device. No subscriptions, no tracking, no nonsense.

---

## What's Inside

**🗒 Calendar & Tasks**
Plan your days with a visual calendar and a simple task list. Add tasks to specific dates, set priorities, attach locations and URLs, and check things off as you go. Everything is right where you left it.

**⟳ Routine Tasks**
Set up daily, weekly, or monthly routines and Vagabond will automatically reset them on schedule. Great for habits, recurring chores, or anything you do regularly.

**🕮 Journal**
Write daily entries, rate your mood and energy, and look back on your week or month with the wrapped view. Your journal is yours — private, local, and always available offline.

**✎ Appearance Customization**
Make Vagabond feel like home. Choose your background, customize panel and card colors, switch between light and dark mode, and pick a font that feels right.

**🗘 Auto-Updates**
When a new version of Vagabond is available, you'll get a prompt right inside the app. No need to manually check for downloads — just click and you're up to date.

## ➜] Download & Install

Head over to the [Releases](https://github.com/yohanceflagerjr/Vagabond/releases) page and grab the latest version for your platform.

### Windows
1. Download `Vagabond_x.x.x_x64-setup.exe`
2. Run the installer and follow the prompts
3. Launch Vagabond from your Start Menu or Desktop

> **Note:** Windows may show a SmartScreen warning since the app isn't from the Microsoft Store. Click **"More info"** → **"Run anyway"** to proceed — the app is safe.

### macOS
1. Download `Vagabond_x.x.x_x64.dmg` (Intel) or `Vagabond_x.x.x_aarch64.dmg` (Apple Silicon — M1/M2/M3)
2. Open the `.dmg` file and drag **Vagabond** into your Applications folder
3. Launch Vagabond from your Applications folder or Spotlight

> **Note:** On first launch macOS may say the app "cannot be opened because the developer cannot be verified." To fix this, go to **System Settings → Privacy & Security** and click **"Open Anyway"** next to the Vagabond entry.

## ⌯✈︎ Getting Started

When you first open Vagabond you'll land on the login screen. Here's how to get going:

1. **Create an account** — tap "Sign Up", choose a username, and optionally set a password or PIN
2. **Set up your profile** — add your name and a profile photo in the settings panel
3. **Add your first task** — tap the **+** button on the Calendar or To Do tab to create a task
4. **Write a journal entry** — switch to the Journal tab and start typing
5. **Make it yours** — head to Settings → Appearance to customize colors, fonts, and background

That's it! Everything saves automatically as you go.

## � FAQ

**Is my data private?**
Yes, completely. All your tasks, journal entries, and settings are stored locally on your device. Nothing is ever sent to a server or the cloud.

**Does it work offline?**
Entirely. Vagabond has no internet requirement — it works the same whether you're connected or not.

**Can multiple people use it on the same device?**
Yes! Vagabond supports multiple accounts on the same device. Each user's data is completely separate from the others.

**What if I get a new computer?**
Use the **Export Backup** feature in Settings → Data & Storage to save your data as a `.json` file, then use **Import Backup** on your new device to restore everything.

**How do I update the app?**
Vagabond will notify you automatically when a new version is available. You can also manually check in Settings → Data & Storage → Check for Updates.

**I'm getting a security warning on Windows/Mac — is the app safe?**
Yes! The warning appears because Vagabond is independently distributed rather than through an app store. See the install instructions above for how to bypass the warning.

**Something isn't working — how do I get help?**
